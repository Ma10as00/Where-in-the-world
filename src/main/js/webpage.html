Where in the world is CS?

Write some coordinates in the texbox below, and they will be formatted into a geoJSON string.

Examples on valid input: 
            
 45.9 S, 170.5 E Dunedin
            
 40° 26.767' N 79° 58.933' W
            
 00010.° 054e0' 12.6000", -63.4457 S Hell
Explanations of the buttons:

'Go to map': Click to display your locations on a map.

'Export': Click to export the geoJSON data to a .txt file.

'Undo': Click to remove the last position from the dataset.

'Clear': Click to clear all geoJSON data, and start over.
